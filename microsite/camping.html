Camping

What do you do after an adventure in the world's best cave experience? That's easy... Explore the unique beauty of southern Arizona via Kartchner Caverns State Park, then stay the night in one of the camping cabins! Hike into the desert backcountry, photograph a variety of birds and wildlife, and then enjoy nearby attractions in Benson, Bisbee, Sierra Vista, and Tombstone. The adventure potential is nearly endless... You bring the mindset, and we'll supply the peace of mind with our air-conditioned cabins.

Cabin Rentals

Two-room camping cabins with a view of the Whetstone Mountains are available at Kartchner, right in the park. You can click below to reserve your cabin online or call us at the Reservations Desk at (877) MY-PARKS. We're here to help you plan your visit to Kartchner Caverns seven days a week from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Arizona time.

Experience all the fun of camping with a little more comfort and a lot less hassle with these convenient southern Arizona cabin rentals. Cabins at Kartchner Caverns State Park provide a perfect destination for families looking for a camping experience without the need for an RV or tent. The cabins are located in the beautiful desert landscape of Kartchner Caverns State Park at the base of the Whetstone Mountains. They are close to hiking trails and just walking distance from the Discovery Center and other park amenities.

Cabin Rates

  • Cabins are $129 per night plus a $5 reservation fee.
  • A refundable $50 cleaning deposit will be taken upon reservation. This deposit will be refunded if the cabin is returned in the condition in which it was reserved.

Cabin Features

  • Queen bed and two twin bunk beds (sleep a maximum of six people)
  • Electricity and air-conditioning/heating
  • Microwave and mini fridge
  • Restrooms and showers within walking distance
  • Pets are allowed in cabins 3 and 4 (with a $10 fee and a limit of two pets per cabin)

Check-in & Check-out

  • Check-in time: 3 p.m. (Arriving earlier does not guarantee availability)
  • Check-out time: 10 a.m. on the scheduled departure date
  • Visitors arriving before 3 p.m. will be charged the park entrance fee.

Additional Activities

During your stay, make sure to take a cave tour, explore the Discovery Center, or enjoy a hike on the park's easy to moderate trails.

Park and Facility Hours

  • Discovery Center: 8:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.
  • Holiday Hours:
    • Thanksgiving: 8:00 a.m. - 2:00 p.m.
    • Christmas Eve: 8:00 a.m. - 2:00 p.m.
    • Christmas: Closed

Park Entrance Fees

  • Per vehicle (1 adult): $10.00
  • Per vehicle (2-4 adults): $20.00
  • Individual/bicycle: $5.00
  • Cave tour tickets are extra.
  • Entrance fee waived for cave tour and campsite reservations.
