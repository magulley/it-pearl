General Questions

General Information Q: Where is Kartchner Caverns State Park located? A: The park is located in southeastern Arizona, near Benson. The address is 2980 AZ-90, Benson, AZ 85602. Q: What are the park’s hours of operation? A: The park is open year-round, but hours vary by season. Check our website or call ahead for current hours. Q: How do I get to Kartchner Caverns? A: The park is easily accessible from Interstate 10, about 9 miles south of Benson on State Route 90.

Cave Tours

Q: What cave tours are available? A: We offer multiple guided tours, including: Rotunda/Throne Room Tour – See the massive column known as Kubla Khan. Big Room Tour – Available October through April (closed for bat maternity season). Helmet & Headlamp Tour – A more adventurous experience. Q: Do I need to make reservations for a cave tour? A: Yes! Reservations are highly recommended, especially on weekends and holidays. You can book online or call the visitor center. Q: Are cameras or cell phones allowed inside the caverns? A: No. To protect the cave’s delicate environment, photography, cell phones, and other electronics are not allowed inside. Q: What should I wear for a cave tour? A: The caverns are humid and stay around 70°F year-round. Wear comfortable, closed-toe shoes and a light jacket if needed. Q: Are the tours wheelchair accessible? A: Yes! The Rotunda/Throne Room Tour is fully accessible. Contact us ahead of time for any special accommodations.

Visitor Information and Policies

Visitor Information & Policies Q: Is there a gift shop or restaurant at the park? A: Yes! The Discovery Center has a gift shop, and the Bat Cave Café offers food and refreshments. Q: Can I bring food and drinks into the caverns? A: No. Food, drinks, and gum are not allowed inside the cave to protect its natural ecosystem. Q: Is Kartchner Caverns family-friendly? A: Absolutely! The park offers kid-friendly tours, an educational Discovery Center, and outdoor activities for all ages. Q: Are group or school tours available? A: Yes! We offer group rates and educational tours for schools and organizations. Call ahead to arrange your visit. Q: What should I do if I have more questions? A: Call our visitor center at (520) 586-2283 or check our website for more details.
