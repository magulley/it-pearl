Cave Tours

Cave tour reservations are strongly encouraged.

Please read through the information below in advance of your visit. The cave environment is unique, and you may need to take special actions to prepare.

Make Cave Tour Reservations and Book Tickets

Reserve online or call (877) MY-PARKS and select Option #2. You can call seven days a week, from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. MST. Group tours should review additional information.

Please arrive AT LEAST 30 minutes prior to your scheduled tour time. For tours starting at 8 a.m., please arrive at least 15 minutes early. Late arrival may result in the forfeiture of your reserved tour time.

Please be advised: video or photography inside the cave is not permitted.

Minors (under age 18) are not allowed on a cave tour without a parent or guardian.

White Nose Syndrome Prevention

Kartchner Caverns is now following established nationwide protocols to prevent the spread of White Nose Syndrome, a fungal disease that is killing bats.

We request that if you have been in another cave or mine since 2005, you do not wear or bring any of the same clothes or shoes as on your other cave trips. If you arrive at Kartchner Caverns wearing shoes that have been in another cave, you will be asked to visit a White Nose Syndrome Decontamination Station, where your shoes will be sprayed down with rubbing alcohol to kill the fungus. Thank you for working with us to protect our bats!

Rotunda/Throne Tour

Length: ½ mile. Time: 1½ hours.

This tour is a half of a mile in length and takes approximately an hour and a half to complete, 50 minutes of which is underground. Discover the role that water plays in creating the caverns. You will see the discoverers' original trail, 45,000-year-old bat guano, delicate formations, and “Kubla Khan,” the largest column formation in Arizona.

Big Room Tour

Length: ½ mile. Time: 1¾ hours.

This tour is available from October 15 to April 15 and is scheduled throughout the day. It is half of a mile long and takes an hour and 45 minutes to complete, one hour of which is underground. The Kartchner Cavern story is about amazing discoveries past, present, and future. Marvel at the many strange and colorful formations. Learn about the cave fauna, both living and ancient. Experience the wonder of the cave’s discovery and learn about new scientific research and discoveries as ongoing studies continue.

Note: Big Room Tours are not available for children under 7 years of age.

Reserve Online

Helmet & Headlamp Tour

Length: ½ mile. Time: 1¼ hours.

Experience the cave as the discoverers did in 1974 using only the light provided by the headlamp on your helmet. The Kartchner Caverns story is about amazing discoveries past, present, and future. Tours are available year-round. From October to April, the tour takes place in the Big Room; the remainder of the year, the tour takes place in the Throne Room/Rotunda. Tour is currently offered only on Saturdays, starting June 26, 2021. Tours are not held on the third Saturday of the month. The tour is half-a-mile long and takes an hour and 15 minutes to complete, one hour of which is underground.

Note: This tour is not available for children under 10 years of age.

Photo Tours

Length: ½ mile. Time: 2 hours.

Have you ever wanted to take photos of the amazing underground world of Kartchner Caverns? Here's your chance! Arizona State Parks and Trails now offers an opportunity to take photos inside the cave every month. Tours are offered every third Saturday of the month. Photos from these tours cannot be used for commercial purposes. For commercial photos of the cave, or photos for media, please contact pio@azstateparks.gov.

Tours start at 4:30 p.m. and end at 7:30 p.m.

Note: This tour is not available for anyone under 18 years of age.

To register, call 877-MY-PARKS and select Option #2. Registration for this tour is $125 per person.
